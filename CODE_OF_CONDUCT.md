# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
The short version:

- Be respectful.
- Assume good faith.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- If someone acts in bad faith, report it to the maintainers and they'll
  deal with it — full text and enforcement guidelines at the link above.

## Reporting

Email [conduct@limehawk.io](mailto:conduct@limehawk.io) for any concerns.
Reports are handled privately and in good faith.
